Our acquaintance:-

Rajshahi Polytechnic Institute was established in 1963 from rajshahi railway station, about 1 km north of Rajshahi railway station, about 1 km north of Rajshahi Airport and Rajshahi-Naogaon Road, in a pleasant environment and beautiful infrastructure, in the memory of the renowned Awliya Hazrat Shah Makhdum (R) of Bangladesh. Rajshahi Polytechnic Institute has a total of 07 buildings and 02 beautiful ponds across a large area, 2 residential student hostels named Shahid Monaim and Shah Neyamat Ullah for the students to live and 01 student hostel named Akhtarun Nessa for the students, auditorium, mosque and playground for prayers. According to the curriculum of the Technical Education Board, there are non-tech departments for teaching technology in mechanical, electrical, civil, electro-medical, power, mechatronics, electronics and computer science and technology and related subjects. Rajshahi Polytechnic Institute has robotics club, debate and language club, rover scout group, youth red crescent society to accelerate co-educational activities.
